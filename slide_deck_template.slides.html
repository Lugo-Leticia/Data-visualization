Prosper Loan Data Visualization¶

Introduction¶

A loan is the lending of money by one or more individuals, organizations, or other entities to other individuals, organization, etc. The recipient (i.e. the borrower) incurs a debt, and is usually liable to pay interest on that debt until it is repaid, and also to repay the principal amount borrowed.This work is provided from Loan Data from Prosper which consist of 113,937 loans with 81 variables, last updated on 03/11/2014. From the 81 variables, only some variables of interest were selected.¶

Most loans borrowers have C credit grade when they opened a loan. We can see how almost the same amount of good and bad credit grade borrowers open a loan in Prosper Loan Company.¶
